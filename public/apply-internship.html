GVMUN Internship Program

GVMUN internships are an exciting opportunity to advance your MUN careers, gain entry-level organizational experience, receive volunteer hours, and gain an opportunity to advance to a full Secretariat member for exemplary work. Note that internships are seasonal, and your application will be considered within the next available cycle.

Available Positions

Outreach Intern

Responsibilities: Email high school and college delegations, contact international thought leaders for interviews, manage sponsor relations, and promote GVMUN on social media platforms.

Skills: Outreach and networking skills, copywriting and email drafting experience, knowledge of Instagram and Canva social media campaigns, creativity and visualization skills.

Journalism Intern

Responsibilities: Research current and historical events for articles and case studies, contribute to trivia initiatives, serve as international press team member at conferences.

Skills: Familiarity with international affairs and UN system, ability to analyze history and geopolitics, effective writing and communication skills, basic research skills.

Research Intern

Responsibilities: Contribute to scholarly policy papers for SSRN submission, write in-depth topic briefs for conference committees like DISEC and SOCHUM.

Skills: Experience with scholarly writing, familiarity with international affairs and geopolitics, ability to analyze history, advanced research skills including database and think tank sources.

What You'll Gain

  • Advance your MUN career with real organizational experience
  • Receive volunteer hours for your service
  • Opportunity to advance to full Secretariat member for exemplary work
  • Direct mentorship from experienced team members
  • Certificate of completion for your portfolio
  • Networking opportunities with global delegates and experts
