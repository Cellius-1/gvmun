GVMUN Internship Program

Our internship program offers students a unique opportunity to gain hands-on experience in international relations, event management, and diplomatic engagement. Work directly with our experienced secretariat team on real-world projects that impact thousands of delegates worldwide.

What You'll Gain

  • Direct mentorship from experienced team members
  • Certificate of completion for your portfolio
  • Networking opportunities with global delegates
  • Portfolio development and career guidance
  • Real-world project management experience
  • Leadership development opportunities

Program Details

Duration

Fall 2025 Program (September - December)

Time Commitment

10-15 hours per week

Eligibility

High school or university students

Location

Remote/Virtual collaboration
