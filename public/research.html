Policy Analysis Published: March 2025

The Evolution of Digital Diplomacy: How Virtual Platforms are Reshaping International Relations

Ayushmaan Mukherjee, Kush Dave, Jackson Dorris

This paper examines the transformative impact of digital platforms on diplomatic practices in the 21st century. Through analysis of virtual summits, online negotiations, and digital public diplomacy initiatives, we explore how technology is fundamentally altering the landscape of international relations. Our findings suggest that virtual diplomacy offers unprecedented opportunities for inclusive global governance while presenting new challenges for diplomatic protocol and security.

Academic Study Published: February 2025

Youth Engagement in Global Governance: The Role of Model UN in Developing Future Leaders

Aayush Dhar, Lilli Stroock, Tharun Naguleswaran

This comprehensive study analyzes the impact of Model United Nations programs on youth development and political engagement. Through surveys of over 1,000 former MUN participants and longitudinal tracking of career outcomes, we demonstrate the significant correlation between MUN participation and future leadership in international affairs. The research provides evidence-based recommendations for enhancing MUN programs to maximize their educational and developmental impact.

Working Paper Published: January 2025

Climate Justice and International Cooperation: Analyzing Equity in Global Environmental Policies

Jackson Dorris, Ayushmaan Mukherjee

This working paper explores the concept of climate justice within the framework of international environmental law and policy. We examine how global climate agreements address issues of equity and fairness, particularly regarding the differential impacts of climate change on developing nations. The analysis includes case studies from recent climate summits and proposes mechanisms for ensuring more equitable distribution of climate mitigation and adaptation responsibilities.

Technical Report Published: December 2024

Best Practices for Virtual Model UN Conferences: A Comprehensive Implementation Guide

Kush Dave, Tharun Naguleswaran, Anushka Chaudhary

Drawing from our experience organizing multiple virtual Model UN conferences, this technical report provides detailed guidance for educators and organizers transitioning to online formats. The report covers platform selection, participant engagement strategies, committee management techniques, and technological solutions for common challenges. Includes practical templates, checklists, and troubleshooting guides based on real-world implementation experiences.
