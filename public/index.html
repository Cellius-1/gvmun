What is Model UN?

Model United Nations, or MUN, is a simulation of the real UN for high school and college students. Delegates go to conferences, where they debate crucial global issues in committees, and collaborate to form resolutions - comprehensive solutions to these problems. It's a versatile platform that pairs talent with passion to spark real change.

Highlights

Training

We will offer live training, videos, and documents - all free of charge - to those new to MUN.

Political Education

We hope to politically educate our delegates in the democratic process, the UN, different forms of governments, and more.

Media

We will strive to be an unbiased source of news on key international events, especially regarding the UN.

Impact

We hope to offer mentorship and essential resources to delegates to spark change in their communities.

Partnerships

To expand our reach and improve quality, we'll secure partnerships with local clubs, organizations, and eventually, UN bodies

Resolutionmaking

Conferences will be productive and impactful through our improved resolutionmaking procedures.
