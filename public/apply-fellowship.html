GVMUN Global Research Fellowship

The GVMUN Global Research Fellowship is a good way to develop basic research and policy analysis skills. As part of this initiative, fellows will attend 8 virtual sessions, where they'll learn the skills of global policy analysis before creating a research-style policy paper of their own. Finally, all fellows will be certified by GVMUN, as well as given the opportunity to advance to become a research internship.

Fellowship Structure

Note: Fellowships are seasonal, and your application will be considered within the next available cycle. The format is as follows, but may be subject to minor changes:

Week 1

Orientation, Global Issues & Topic Selection

Week 2

Research Methods & Source Building

Week 3

Issue Framing & Case Study Analysis

Week 4

Outlining & Structuring the Paper

Week 5

Writing Part I: Introduction & Background

Week 6

Writing Part II: Body & Analysis

Week 7

Writing Part III: Conclusion & Peer Review

Week 8

Finalization & Presentation

All sessions will generally be held in the morning.

Fellowship Benefits

  • Learn research methods and policy analysis skills
  • Attend 8 structured virtual sessions
  • Create your own research-style policy paper
  • Receive GVMUN certification upon completion
  • Opportunity to advance to research internship
  • Access to exclusive research mentorship

Program Details

Duration

8-week program

Time Commitment

5-10 hours per week

Prerequisites

Interest in policy and global issues

Outcome

Research paper & GVMUN certification
