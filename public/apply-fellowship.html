GVMUN Fellowship Program

Our prestigious fellowship program is designed for exceptional students who demonstrate outstanding leadership potential and want to take on significant responsibilities in global advocacy and Model UN education. Fellows lead major initiatives, conduct research, and help shape the future of GVMUN.

Fellowship Benefits

  • Lead research projects and policy initiatives
  • Mentor interns and other participants
  • Present at international conferences
  • Access to exclusive leadership training
  • Direct collaboration with Secretariat
  • Publication opportunities in GVMUN research
  • Letter of recommendation upon completion

Program Details

Duration

Fall 2025 Program (September - December)

Time Commitment

15-20 hours per week

Prerequisites

Previous MUN experience strongly preferred
