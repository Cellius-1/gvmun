GVMUNEC IV 2025 - Essay Competition

Debates. Ideas. Solutions.

GVMUN is pleased to announce the fourth iteration of the Global Virtual Model United Nations Essay Competition (GVMUNEC), a contest for aspiring writers and thinkers to share their insight on the world. The competition will be composed of essay submissions from an informed international audience looking to spark change in the world.

Choose One Prompt:

Prompt 1: Artificial Intelligence

How should the international community address the geopolitical and ethical challenges posed by artificial intelligence?

Prompt 2: Climate Equity

How can the international community ensure climate equity by addressing the disproportionate impacts of climate change on vulnerable populations?

Requirements:

  • 600-800 word essay answering one prompt
  • Times New Roman, size 12 font
  • Submit as PDF with format: [Name] - [Topic Number] - [Word Count]
  • Works cited page recommended (mandatory if citations are used)

Important Dates:

  • Applications Open: May 19, 2025
  • Submission Deadline: July 27, 2025
  • Competition Date: July 27, 2025

Awards & Recognition:

  • Top 3 essays from each category receive awards
  • Recognition on social media platforms
  • Opportunity to become GVMUN Interns
  • Work directly with our team
