Learn about our mission, vision, and what makes us unique

What is Model UN?

Model United Nations, or MUN, is a simulation of the real UN for high school and college students. Delegates go to conferences, where they debate crucial global issues in committees, and collaborate to form resolutions - comprehensive solutions to these problems. It's a versatile platform that pairs talent with passion to spark real change.

Our Mission

We're the Global Virtual Model United Nations. And we're looking to improve the Model UN space while encouraging youth activism and political education. We believe in creating opportunities for young people to engage with global issues and develop their diplomatic skills.

Our Accomplishments

Global Reach

We've successfully brought together delegates from over 30 countries, creating a truly international experience.

Student Engagement

Over 1300 delegates have participated in our conferences, gaining valuable diplomatic and leadership skills.

Policy Innovation

Our research team has produced 3 major publications on the digital divide and SDG 4, Syrian and Middle Eastern security, and the reconstruction of Haiti, contributing to policy innovation in international relations.

Educational Impact

Our training programs and resources have helped countless students develop their understanding of international relations.
