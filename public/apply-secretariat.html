GVMUN Secretariat Positions

Join the core leadership team that drives GVMUN's mission forward. Secretariat members are the senior decision-makers who shape organizational strategy, lead major conferences, and represent GVMUN globally. This is the highest level of responsibility within our organization.

Secretariat Responsibilities

  • Core decision-making and strategic planning
  • Lead conference committees and sessions
  • Develop organizational policies and procedures
  • Represent GVMUN at external events and partnerships
  • Mentor fellows, interns, and other team members
  • Oversee budget and resource allocation
  • Drive organizational growth and innovation

Available Positions

Director of Conferences

Lead conference planning, committee oversight, and delegate experience

Director of Outreach

Manage partnerships, recruitment, and external relationships

Director of Communications

Oversee marketing, social media, and public relations

Director of Research

Lead research initiatives, publications, and policy analysis

Requirements & Commitment

Duration

1-year commitment minimum
